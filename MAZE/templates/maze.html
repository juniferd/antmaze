What is this?

This is a simulation of ants traversing a maze trying to find food and then deliver the food to the nearest maze exit. Ants drop a chemical marker that they can use to navigate and communicate with other ants.

Initially ants are in explore mode where they try to cover as much territory as possible.

If ants find an exit before they find food, they enter find food mode. They don't mind backtracking and going over already searched through territory.

If ants find the food source before they find an exit, they enter find exit mode. At this point, other ants who find their chemical trail leading to food will jump on the trail and begin collecting food as well.

Once ants have established a path from food to an exit, they'll be in retrieve food mode where they march back and forth between food to exit. Once the food source is depleted, a new food source is placed in the maze and the search continues.
